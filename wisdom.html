Spiritual & Philosophical Insights

On Meditation & Practice

  • Meditation in midst of activity is 1000 times superior to meditation in stillness
  • The mind should be kept in the heart until it reaches the highest end - this is wisdom and liberation
  • You can make any activity into meditation by being completely present with it

On Self & Consciousness

  • Your nature is peace and happiness - there is no reason to be depressed
  • The self cannot be found in books - you have to find it yourself
  • You are the universe expressing itself for a little while

On Love & Relationships

  • Love is what we are - we don't get it from somebody, can't give it to anybody
  • Love can attain what the intellect cannot fathom
  • Attachment is love polluted by selfishness

On Freedom & Surrender

  • We don't work to become free - we simply are free
  • "I don't mind what happens" - that is the essence of inner freedom
  • Release attachment to outcomes - deep inside, you will feel good no matter what

On Learning & Growth

  • Learning is never cumulative - it is a movement of knowing with no beginning and no end
  • Real learning comes about when the competitive spirit has ceased
  • Wisdom comes from practice - find what works for you and keep doing it

On Life's Journey

  • Everyone has their own path - walk yours with integrity and wish others peace on their journey
  • The journey is uniquely yours - you cannot imitate somebody else's path and be true to yourself
  • Where the attention goes, the energy flows

On Mind & Thoughts

  • A free mind doesn't create problems - it meets them and answers them
  • Nothing is a problem until you make it one
  • Relinquish thoughts when not in use

On Living Fully

  • You can't live fully until you can live fully now
  • Approaching life with a sense of adventure is always available
  • Joy is exactly what's happening minus our attempt to control it

On Resilience

  • Ever tried? Ever failed? Doesn't matter. Try again, fail again, fail better
  • Defeat is a state of mind - no one is ever defeated until defeat has been accepted as reality
  • The less effort, the faster and more powerful you will be

Application in decison making and practical life

Learning and Education

True learning requires a mind free from the burden of accumulated knowledge. In the state of absolute silence, we can truly listen and learn.

While we spend most of our lives learning to earn a living, we often neglect actual living. School should be a place of leisure where genuine learning happens without pressure.

Decision Making and Intuition

When intuition speaks, there is no reasoning - it simply feels right. The key is to be present without rushing to answers. This is surrender.

The mind seeks complexity but truth is simple. You cannot reach simplicity through complexity.

Understanding the Self and Body

The belief that the body needs an "I" to function is a fundamental error. The body operates perfectly well without the conscious "I" and its limited processing capabilities.

We are not our bodies. Attachment to physical appearance and society's ideals of beauty can create great suffering. True freedom comes from transcending body identification.

Free Will and Surrender

As Einstein said: "Everything is determined by forces over which we have no control. We all dance to a mysterious tune, intoned in the distance by an invisible piper."

The realization that we don't have free will is actually liberating. Life becomes easier and more wonderful when we stop believing we're in control.

Practical Wisdom

  • Let go gradually:
    • If you let go a little, you get a little happiness
    • If you let go more, you get more happiness
    • If you let go completely, you become free

Meditation and Practice

A powerful approach to surrender is saying "yes" during meditation, especially in difficult situations. Accept things as they are without resistance.

Gradually attain stillness through steady practice. Fix the mind in the Self, understanding that "The Self alone is all; there is nothing apart from It."

Career and Work

You are not bound by any job - jobs come and pass through you while you remain present in the moment. What happens is not your business; being 100% present is what matters.

The Universe will guide your career path - you can't make a "wrong" choice. Stay open to possibilities that may differ from your expectations.
